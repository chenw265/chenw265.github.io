Data Download

  • Enter into the TCGA database website, click the “Repository”；

  • Then find the buttons “Files” and “Cases” in the right column, pick up the data you want;

  • After that, click dark blue button on the center “Add All Files to Cart”, add your data to the Cart;

  • Click ”Cart” on the top right corner, then you will go to the “Cart”, where you can download your data;

  • Then click ”Download”, there will give you to options: ”Manifest“ and ”Cart”;

  • If your data is not so big, you could click ”Cart” and download your data directly. But if your data is too big to download, you should choose ”Manifest“, you can use it download the data with gdc data transfer tool, which can be downloaded in TCGA website;
